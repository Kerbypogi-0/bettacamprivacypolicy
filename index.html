Privacy Policy for Betta Cam

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Betta Cam. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application, developed by Kerby Beronilla.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: To create an account and save your tracking data, we collect your email address and a secure password.

Camera Access: The application requires access to your device's camera (android.permission.CAMERA). This is strictly necessary for the core functionality of scanning and identifying different types of Betta fish using our on-device AI model.

3. How We Use Your Information

Authentication & Data Sync: We use Supabase, a secure backend service provider, to handle your authentication. Your email is used strictly for account creation, secure login, and syncing your personal aquarium data across devices.

Image Processing: The camera feed is processed locally on your device to identify fish types. We do not upload, share, or store your live camera feed or photos on external servers without your explicit action.

4. Data Security

Your email and account data are securely stored in our Supabase instance in accordance with industry-standard security and encryption protocols. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at beronillak11@gmail.com.
