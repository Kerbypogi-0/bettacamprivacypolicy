Privacy Policy for Betta Cam

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Betta Cam mobile application. This app was created for educational and scientific identification purposes.

2. Camera Usage

The app requires access to your device's camera to analyze images of Betta fish in real-time. All image processing (AI neural network analysis) is executed entirely locally on your device. We do not record, collect, store, or transmit your live camera feed or photos to any external servers.

3. Local Storage

The app uses your device's local storage to save your scan history (images, names, and confidence scores) to display in the Dashboard. This data remains strictly on your personal device and is never uploaded, harvested, or shared by us.

4. Third-Party Services (Advertisements)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements to support development. Google AdMob may collect and use certain data (such as your device's advertising ID and general location) to provide personalized ads. You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services by visiting Google's official Privacy & Terms site.
